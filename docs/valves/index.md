# Valve Grading Reference

Quick-reference severity grading for stenosis and regurgitation. Based on ACC/AHA and ASE guidelines.

---

## Aortic Stenosis

<div class="grading-table-wrap" markdown="1">

| Severity | V<sub>max</sub> (m/s) | Mean Gradient (mmHg) | AVA (cm²) | AVAi (cm²/m²) | DI |
|----------|----------------------|----------------------|-----------|----------------|----|
| **Mild** | 2.0–2.9 | <25 | >1.5 | >0.85 | >0.50 |
| **Moderate** | 3.0–3.9 | 25–39 | 1.0–1.5 | 0.60–0.85 | 0.25–0.50 |
| **Severe** | ≥4.0 | ≥40 | <1.0 | <0.60 | <0.25 |
| **Very Severe** | ≥5.0 | ≥60 | <0.6 | — | — |

</div>

!!! tip "Low-Flow Low-Gradient AS"
    - **Classic LFLG:** EF <50%, AVA <1.0 cm², mean gradient <40 mmHg → dobutamine stress echo
    - **Paradoxical LFLG:** EF ≥50%, AVA <1.0 cm², mean gradient <40 mmHg, SVi <35 mL/m²
    - CT planimetry of valve area helpful in discordant cases

**AVA by continuity equation:** \(AVA = \frac{CSA_{LVOT} \times VTI_{LVOT}}{VTI_{AV}}\)

## Aortic Regurgitation (CMR Grading)

<div class="grading-table-wrap" markdown="1">

| Severity | RF (%) | RVol (mL/beat) | ERO (cm²) | Vena Contracta (mm) |
|----------|--------|-----------------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Mild** | <20 | <30 | <0.10 | <3 |
| **Moderate** | 20–39 | 30–59 | 0.10–0.29 | 3–6 |
| **Severe** | ≥40 | ≥60 | ≥0.30 | >6 |

</div>

!!! note "CMR AR Assessment"
    Regurgitant fraction by 4D flow or 2D PC at the ascending aorta is the reference standard. RF ≥40% = severe. Also measure LV EDVi, ESVi, and EF.

**Aortic root dilation thresholds by CT/CMR:**

| Site | Normal | Consider surgery |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Aortic annulus | <25 mm/m² | — |
| Sinus of Valsalva | ♂ <40 mm, ♀ <36 mm | ♂ ≥55 mm (≥50 mm in Marfan/bicuspid) |
| Ascending aorta | <40 mm | ≥55 mm (≥45 mm in bicuspid) |

## Mitral Stenosis

<div class="grading-table-wrap" markdown="1">

| Severity | MVA — PHT (cm²) | MVA — Planimetry (cm²) | Mean Gradient (mmHg) | PASP (mmHg) |
|----------|-----------------|------------------------|----------------------|-------------|
| **Mild** | >1.5 | >1.5 | <5 | <30 |
| **Moderate** | 1.0–1.5 | 1.0–1.5 | 5–10 | 30–50 |
| **Severe** | <1.0 | <1.0 | >10 | >50 |

</div>

!!! tip "Wilkins Score"
    Echocardiographic score for MitraClip/PBMV suitability: leaflet mobility, thickening, calcification, subvalvular thickening. Score ≤8 = favorable for PBMV.

## Mitral Regurgitation (CMR Grading)

<div class="grading-table-wrap" markdown="1">

| Severity | RF (%) | RVol (mL/beat) | ERO (cm²) | Vena Contracta (mm) |
|----------|--------|-----------------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Mild** | <20 | <30 | <0.20 | <3 |
| **Moderate** | 20–39 | 30–59 | 0.20–0.39 | 3–6.9 |
| **Severe** | ≥40 | ≥60 | ≥0.40 | ≥7 |

</div>

**CMR method:** LV stroke volume − Ao net forward flow = MR regurgitant volume.
Or direct: PC at mitral valve (less reliable due to valve motion).

## Tricuspid Regurgitation

<div class="grading-table-wrap" markdown="1">

| Severity | Vena Contracta (mm) | ERO (cm²) | RVol (mL/beat) | RVSP |
|----------|---------------------|-----------|-----------------|------|
| **Mild** | <3 | <0.20 | <30 | Normal |
| **Moderate** | 3–6.9 | 0.20–0.39 | 30–44 | Borderline |
| **Severe** | ≥7 | ≥0.40 | ≥45 | Elevated |
| **Massive** | ≥9 | ≥0.60 | ≥60 | — |

</div>

**RVSP estimation (TR jet):** RVSP = 4 × V²<sub>TR</sub> + RAP

*RAP estimate: 3 mmHg (normal IVC), 8 mmHg (borderline), 15 mmHg (dilated, non-collapsing IVC)*

## Pulmonary Regurgitation (CMR Grading)

<div class="grading-table-wrap" markdown="1">

| Severity | RF (%) | RVol (mL/beat) |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| **Mild** | <20 | <30 |
| **Moderate** | 20–39 | 30–44 |
| **Severe** | ≥40 | ≥45 |

</div>

!!! note
    CMR RF by 2D PC or 4D flow at the main PA is the clinical reference for PR severity, especially in post-TOF repair follow-up.
